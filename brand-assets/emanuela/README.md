# Laborator Emanuela brand assets

Curated assets downloaded from the official [Laborator Emanuela website](https://laboratoremanuela.ro/) on 2026-06-19. Use these files for the Emanuela rebrand across `site`, `mobile`, `webadmin`, and `landingpage-proposal` instead of searching for replacements.

- [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/laboratoremanuela/)
- [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/laboratoremanuela/)

## Visual direction

The official identity combines a vivid raspberry-pink wordmark, a warm champagne seal, plum details, dark text, and generous light space. A practical UI palette derived from that identity is:

- Raspberry: `#E92D79`
- Champagne: `#F2BF78`
- Plum: `#862875`
- Warm ink: `#241A1C`
- Ivory: `#FFF8F2`
- Blush: `#F9E4E7`

Use the colors as design tokens, not as equal-area blocks. Keep backgrounds mostly ivory/light, use raspberry for primary actions, champagne for restrained accents, and warm ink for readable text.

## Asset guide

### Logos

- `logos/logo-primary.webp`: primary full-color transparent logo; preferred for light backgrounds.
- `logos/logo-header-light.png`: white mark for dark or photographic backgrounds.
- `logos/logo-email.png`: compact horizontal lockup; use only when the primary logo is too wide.
- `logos/logo-legacy-mark.png`: older vertical mark; reference/secondary use only.
- `logos/favicon.jpg`: official square favicon and app-icon reference.

### Hero photography

- `hero/kids-sugar-free.jpg`: wide product photograph for children's products.
- `hero/events-wide.jpeg` and `hero/events-mobile.jpg`: event/candy-bar imagery for responsive layouts.
- `hero/birthday-wide.jpg` and `hero/birthday-mobile.jpg`: birthday cake imagery with useful negative space.
- `hero/anniversary-wide.jpg` and `hero/anniversary-mobile.jpg`: personalized children's celebration imagery. Crop or avoid visible customer names in reusable UI.

### Categories

- `categories/cake-citrus.jpg`: bright premium cake composition.
- `categories/cake-chocolate.jpg`: chocolate cake and slice composition.
- `categories/monoportions.jpg`: colorful monoporții/product category.
- `categories/candy-bar.jpg`: assorted candy-bar products.
- `categories/delivery.jpg`: delivery/free-delivery section.
